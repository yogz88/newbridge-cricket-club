About

Newbridge Cricket Club is a community-led initiative working with local councillors and Kildare County Council to secure a cricket ground. We’ll affiliate with Cricket Leinster and run junior and senior cricket.

Community

Inclusive, family-friendly, and open to beginners.

Pathways

Soft-ball festivals, coaching, and league teams as we grow.

Safeguarding

Child-first policies and trained volunteers.
