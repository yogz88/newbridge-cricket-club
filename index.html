Bring Cricket to Newbridge

We’re building the case for Newbridge to have its own cricket ground. Step 1: gather community support. Step 2: meet with councillors and Kildare County Council. Step 3: form a registered club and affiliate with Cricket Leinster.

Sign the Petition

What We’re Planning

1. Petition

Show the council that Newbridge wants cricket. Add your name today.

2. A Ground with a Mat

A council-supported cricket ground with an artificial wicket suitable for both tennis-ball and leather-ball cricket.

3. Facilities

Open access for juniors, families, and festivals. Basic equipment storage and safe boundaries.

Our Roadmap

  1. Community Support: Collect signatures on the petition.
  2. Council Engagement: Meet councillors and Kildare County Council to secure a site.
  3. Form the Club: Elect interim committee, adopt constitution.
  4. Cricket Leinster: Affiliate for governance, insurance, and competitive pathways.

About This Initiative

We are residents of Newbridge and nearby areas, working to provide a local, inclusive cricket option for kids and adults. With your support, Newbridge can have its own cricket ground and a club that thrives for generations.
