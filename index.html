What We Need

A Ground

A council-supported site in Newbridge, safe and accessible for the whole community.

A Playing Surface

An artificial cricket mat/wicket so we can host tennis-ball and leather-ball cricket.

Facilities

Open access for juniors, families, and festivals. Basic equipment storage and safe boundaries.

Our Plan

  1. Community Support: Collect signatures on the petition.
  2. Council Engagement: Meet councillors and Kildare County Council to secure a site.
  3. Form the Club: Elect interim committee, adopt constitution.
  4. Cricket Leinster: Affiliate for governance, insurance, and competitive pathways.

Accreditation & Facilities (What’s Expected)

For league cricket under Cricket Leinster, clubs are typically expected to have:

  • A safe, well-maintained ground with a suitable wicket (we propose an artificial cricket mat that supports both tennis-ball and leather-ball cricket).
  • Toilets (shared park toilets are acceptable initially).
  • Basic clubhouse or shelter & secure storage for equipment.
  • Governance & finance: Chair, Secretary, Treasurer, plus an independent auditor to review annual accounts.

Preferred path for Newbridge: partner with Kildare County Council in an existing park so the council provides ground maintenance and the club contributes via rental/maintenance fees.
