Let’s Build Cricket in Newbridge

We’re forming Newbridge’s own cricket club—open to adults and juniors, beginners and league players. Add your name, support our petition, and help us secure a local ground.

What We Need

A Ground

A council-supported site in Newbridge, safe and accessible for the whole community.

A Playing Surface

An artificial cricket mat/wicket so we can host tennis-ball and leather-ball cricket.

Facilities

Open access for juniors, families, and festivals. Basic equipment storage and safe boundaries.

What We’re Planning

Ground in Newbridge

Working with local councillors and Kildare County Council to secure a cricket-ready pitch.

Junior & Senior Cricket

Pathways for kids and adults. From soft-ball festivals to competitive league teams in Cricket Leinster.

Coaching & Inclusivity

Level-1 coaches and safeguarding standards. We welcome everyone—no experience required.

Our Plan

  1. Community Support: Collect signatures on the petition.
  2. Council Engagement: Meet councillors and Kildare County Council to secure a site.
  3. Form the Club: Elect interim committee, adopt constitution.
  4. Cricket Leinster: Affiliate for governance, insurance, and competitive pathways.

About This Initiative

We are residents of Newbridge and nearby areas working to provide a local, inclusive cricket option for kids and adults. With your support, Newbridge can have its own cricket club and ground for generations to come.
