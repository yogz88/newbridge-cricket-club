Bring Cricket to Newbridge

We’re a local group working to secure a cricket ground in Newbridge, Co. Kildare. Step 1 is community support. Step 2 is meeting the councillor and Kildare County Council to identify a site. Step 3 is forming the club and affiliating with Cricket Leinster.

What We Need in Newbridge

  • A council-supported ground site in Newbridge suitable for community cricket.
  • Cricket playing surface: an artificial cricket mat / wicket so we can host tennis-ball and leather-ball cricket.
  • Open access for juniors, families, and community festivals.
  • Basic storage for equipment and safe boundary space.

Our Plan

  1. Community Support: Collect signatures and suggestions (this page).
  2. Meet the Councillor / Council: Present the petition and request help identifying a suitable ground in Newbridge.
  3. Form the Club: Elect interim committee (Chair, Secretary, Treasurer, Safeguarding), adopt a simple constitution.
  4. Affiliate with Cricket Leinster: Governance, insurance, coaching support, and entry to competitions as we grow.

We will publish meeting dates and progress updates here.

About This Initiative

We are residents of Newbridge and nearby areas working to provide a local, inclusive cricket option for kids and adults. If you want to help, please sign the petition and leave your suggestion.
