What We Need

  • A council-supported ground in Newbridge
  • An artificial cricket mat/wicket for tennis-ball and leather-ball cricket
  • Basic facilities: toilets, storage, safe boundaries

Our Plan

  1. Gather community support through petition
  2. Engage with Kildare County Council
  3. Form the club with interim committee
  4. Affiliate with Cricket Leinster

Accreditation

To participate in Cricket Leinster leagues, clubs need:

  • A safe, well-maintained ground
  • Toilets and basic clubhouse
  • Governance: Chair, Secretary, Treasurer, Auditor

About the Club

Newbridge Cricket Club is a community-led initiative. Unlike Naas and North Kildare, Newbridge has no official club. We aim to unite local groups and build a lasting cricket community.
